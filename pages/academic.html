University of Waterloo is a public research university with a main campus in Waterloo, Ontario. The university was established on 1 July 1957, during the 60 years, the UW has became one of the best universities of Canada. University of Waterloo is known for its co-op programme, which allows students to gain applicable work experiences during their eduction.

In QS's 2018 graduate employability ranking, Waterloo hold the second place in Canada and 24th in the world. The University has six faculties and ten faculty-based schools.
